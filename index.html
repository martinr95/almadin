Seceda

Seceda captivates with breathtaking beauty. Towering peaks and lush meadows surround this alpine haven. Immerse yourself in its splendor through hiking, skiing, and cable car rides. Unveil nature's grandeur at every turn, creating an unforgettable mountain experience.

Alpe di Siusi

Alpe di Siusi, a gem in Val Gardena, unfolds as an expansive meadow framed by majestic peaks. Activities abound: from hiking and biking to the thrill of paragliding. Ascend to panoramic vistas via cable car and savor outdoor adventures in this alpine paradise that promises a serene escape.

Rasciesa

Rasciesa enchants with its serene charm. Nestled amidst rolling hills and dense forests, it offers a haven for hikers and nature lovers. Explore scenic trails, savor panoramic views, and embrace the tranquility. Rasciesa invites you to unwind in its alpine embrace, a retreat for those seeking beauty and solace.

Col Raiser

Col Raiser, in the heart of Val Gardena, is a mountain haven surrounded by rugged peaks. Embark on a cable car journey to unveil sweeping vistas and tranquility. This alpine retreat beckons outdoor enthusiasts, offering an immersive experience amidst nature's grandeur, a perfect sanctuary for those seeking both adventure and serenity.

Holiday apartments in Ortisei and Selva in Val Gardena

Great comfort and a warm welcome await you in our perfectly located apartments– holidays in Val Gardena in the Dolomites

I’m Andreas Runggaldier and I’m pleased to welcome You to our apartments. By now I’ve more than 10 years of experience as a host and I’m convinced that the job is all about having a passion for hospitality and the desire to offer Your guests the best and most authentic stay possible. However, I started my professional career as a goldsmith and after my diploma I decided to open a laboratory in my hometown, Ortisei, but not long after I got the opportunity to travel all over the world for work and as a head of jury for the “World Skills”. While travelling I had the chance to meet a lot of great hosts and to see what good hospitality is like, but also how it’s not done. All those experiences came in handy when we opened our first apartments in Selva, in 2009. From there on, i discovered my passion for this job and with the success we had in the next couple of years, we got the opportunity to manage four more apartments in the "Cesa Setil" in Ortisei and after that, in 2019 we were entrusted with the management oft the apartments "Cesa Petlin" in Ortisei. From there on, I decided to focus solely and passionately on the job as a host and manager and to care for the complete well being of our guests, because I know that an unforgettable stay starts with genuine and authentic hospitality
